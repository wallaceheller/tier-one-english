About Me.


A Brief Brief.

I'm not a teacher—I'm a writer, a fiend with grammar and usage. From the quirks to the sublime, I get English, and I can help you get it too—better than ever. My interests include art, advertising, media, and design. I love film and fiction, the darker the better. I'm from New York City originally, but I've lived in Chicago, LA, and more places than you can count. I've got a Bachelor's Degree in Behavioral Science, a certificate in filmmaking, and a passion for making a difference where it matters most.
